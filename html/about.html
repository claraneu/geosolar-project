About Geosolar

The story behind bringing the sunshine into your home

We are a group of University students based in Copenhagen that collaborated on a tech project in the autumn semester of 2020. Our aim is to help you decide on whether an investment into solar energy is worth your while, especially in weather-sensitive Denmark.

All of us started on a different learning track provided by the student organisation Techlabs. Applying our learnings in the field of Data Science, UX Design and Web Development enabled us to create this project. If you are student and would like to hear more about how you can create projects like this, check out Techlabs Copenhagen.
